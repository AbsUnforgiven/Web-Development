UNORDRED LISTS


Unordered lists Unordered (bulleted) lists are used when a set of items can be placed in any order. An example is a shopping list-
milk
bread
butter
coffee beans
Although the items are all part of one list, you could put the items in any order and the list would still make sense-
bread
coffee beans
milk
butter
Unordered lists use one set of'ul' tags wrapped around one or more sets of 'li' tags. Example-
  • bread
  • coffee beans
  • milk
  • butter
